Total Confirmed Cases

Total confirmed cases: total number of patients tested positive for COVID-19 out of all tests conducted in the country*
At any point in time this figure consists of active cases, total recoveries and the death toll.

*The true number of people historically infected by COVID-19 is likely much higher due to unreported cases, asymptomatic cases and errors in data collection.
Data source: CSSE, Johns Hopkins University

Growth Rate

Growth factor: ratio of new cases today to new cases yesterday
A growth factor of less than 1 means the number of new cases each day is declining.
The growth factor line shown in this graph is calculated using a 5-day moving average.

Data source: CSSE, Johns Hopkins University
